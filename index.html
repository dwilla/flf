About Us

Fourth Light Farm is dedicated to cultivating healthy soil, nourishing communities, and sharing the joy of sustainable agriculture. We believe that the foundation of a thriving ecosystem starts beneath our feet.

Through education, hands-on projects, and outreach, we aim to inspire a deeper connection to the land and empower individuals and communities to embrace sustainable practices.

How We Can Help

Together, we can create a sustainable future.

Community Partnerships

We work with schools, community gardens, and local residents to share sustainable farming practices and build stronger connections to the land.

Educational Workshops

Our workshops and resources empower people to grow their own food, compost effectively, and understand the vital role of soil health.

Hands-On Learning

We make soil science easy to understand through simple activities, farm tours, and interactive classroom visits that inspire all ages.
